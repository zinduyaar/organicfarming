About Us

Our story

The Food India Company (TFIC) is a food production and processing company. It was conceptualized by a family with agricultural roots to provide safe organic and nutritious food with minimal environmental footprint.

Mission

Our mission is to encourage sustainable agro farming practices, support local farmers, and streamline and modernize the prevailing agricultural business models to create a healthy ecosystem for all.

Our timeline

  • Aug 2018
  • May 2019
  • Sep 2020
  • May 2021

Conceptualized TFIC

Months of discussion on the need to organize and support sustainable farming and processing practices in rural India lead to the inception of The Food India Company.

Started land preparation

Operations begin with organic farming under TFIC's first subsidiary, Kheteorganic. Cleared a small piece of ancestral land in the Gangetic plains and left it fallow for a year to allow the natural restoration of nutrients. Meanwhile, arranged the basic infrastructure like electricity connection and tubewell.

Harvested the first crop, paddy

Focused on a single crop to understand the process and learn the techniques; followed it with five crops in the Rabi season.

Licencing and registration

Got Fssai licence and registered Kheteorganic to distribute our products. Conducted consultations to create awareness around organic products and support farmers to begin organic farming.
